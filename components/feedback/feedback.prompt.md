Trust & compliance signals — the credibility backbone of the brand.

```jsx
<Badge variant="accent" icon="map-pin">Nationwide network</Badge>
<Badge variant="success" icon="check">No-obligation</Badge>

<LicenseBadge number="PA-1098842" state="Florida Public Adjuster" />
<LicenseBadge inverse compact />

<DisclaimerCallout title="We represent property owners">
  Adjustly's licensed public adjusters advocate for policyholders, never insurance
  companies. This page is educational and not legal advice.
</DisclaimerCallout>
<DisclaimerCallout variant="warning" title="Deadlines matter">
  Most policies limit how long you have to file. Don't wait to document your loss.
</DisclaimerCallout>
```

- **LicenseBadge** — only show with a real, verifiable license number; attribute experience to the licensed principal, not the new brand.
- **DisclaimerCallout** — drop near any claims advice; keep "free" genuinely free and never imply a guaranteed outcome.
- **Badge** — categories, coverage areas, statuses. Mono, uppercase, factual.
